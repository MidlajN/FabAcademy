About Us

Why Learn At Super Fablab Kochi?

Super Fablab Kochi is a unique establishment where you can see the synthesis of man and machine to bring about dynamic result in tech learning as well as in product development.
Joining Fab Academy at the Super FabLab Kochi gives you the chance to be a part of this philosophy and to learn directly from our talented experts, who have guided and mentored FabAcademy students from 2016 onwards.
